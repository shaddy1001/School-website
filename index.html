Our Co-curricular
Activities

   Fine art


Fine art clubs in schools provide a space for students to explore and develop their creativity and artistic abilities. Members can learn various art techniques and mediums, such as painting, drawing, sculpture, and printmaking, and gain an understanding of art history and different styles and movements

   Creative Writing


A creative writing club in a school is a group of students who come together to learn, practice and improve their writing skills in a supportive and collaborative environment. The club provides a space for students to explore their creativity, express their ideas and experiment with different writing styles and genres.

   Robotics


A robotics club in a school is a group where students learn about designing, building, and programming robots. Students work collaboratively to create robots that can perform various tasks, such as navigating through an obstacle course or completing a specific challenge.
